Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions, answered here

What is Doggy Day Camp?

Doggy Day Camp is a fun and safe place for your furry friend to socialize, play, and relax under the supervision of trained staff while you're away.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Yes! We encourage all pet parents to make a reservation ahead of time to ensure we have a spot for your pup.

What are the drop-off and pick-up times?

Drop-off begins at 7:00 AM and pick-up runs until 7:00 PM. Late pick-ups may incur an additional fee.

What should I bring with my dog?

Just bring your dog on a leash! We'll provide food, water, toys, and love. If your dog has special dietary needs, bring their food and feeding instructions.

Are vaccinations required?

Yes, all dogs must be up-to-date on Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella vaccines. Proof is required before attending.

Can puppies attend?

Puppies must be at least 4 months old and have completed their initial vaccination series to attend.

Do you separate dogs by size or temperament?

Yes, dogs are grouped based on size, play style, and personality to ensure a safe and fun experience for everyone.
